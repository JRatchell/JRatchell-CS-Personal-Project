This project is intended to a sort of stopgap solution for timeless issue of "How exactly do I make doing large amounts of school work (at least somewhat) fun?"
And so, this idea of using the Pomodoro method to create a positive feedback loop of
completing a certain acmount of work, playing a game for a certain amount of time, and repeating that loop ad nauseam came into fruition.
If the sitemap of this site is a diagram of its skeleton, then this (As of 3/08/23) is that same skeleton but with a nice shirt somewhat covering up the ugly parts.
